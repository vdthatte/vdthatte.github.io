I graduated from NYU during May 2018 with a degree in Computer Science and I'm working on my own company, Graam. Graam's mission is to mobilize creators all over the world to share skills with people in their community. My areas of expertise include product design, sales and outreach, marketing, branding, full stack web development, native app development, and much much more. Considering that I have to do all of these being the only person on my team. & I absolutely love it.

I started making apps during the winter break of freshman year. I remember being glued to the screen all winter when it was snowing outside, trying to get things to work. By the end of my one month of winter break, I had a simple app. This was only the beginning, within a few months I found myself freelancing, and interning as an iOS developer. I still continue to freelance and launch my own products to this day which really helps me improve my skills as a programmer.

I'm addicted to coffee and running. When I'm not working on Graam or hanging with people, I'm usually making music.
